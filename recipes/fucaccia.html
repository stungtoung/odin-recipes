Focaccia Bread

This Focaccia Bread recipe is so easy that you'll make it again and again

It takes minimum effort just a lot of inactive time

Focaccia Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this focaccia bread recipe

  • Wheat Flour.
  • Water.
  • Yeast.
  • Olive Oil.
  • Salt.
  • Sugar.
  • Seasoning:Flaky Salt, Rosemary, Thyme.

What to do ?

Here is what you're going to do.

  1. Combine wheat flour, water, yeast, olive oil, sugar and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cover the Bowl and leave it in the fridge overnight.
  3. After 12 hour minimum in the fridge take it out and transfer the dough to baking pan.
  4. leave it to rise in the pan at room temp for another hour.
  5. create dimples in the dough.
  6. drizzle olive oil on the dough "Be generous" and add your seasoning.
  7. Bake it in the oven till golden Brown.
  8. Enjoy! "After it cools down of course".
