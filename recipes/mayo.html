Mayonnaise

Mayonnaise is one of the fundamental condiments used in a variety of other sauces and recipes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs.
  • Neutral Oil.
  • Mustard.
  • Lemon Juice.
  • Salt.

What to do ?

Here is what you're going to do:

  1. Combine the ingredients in a tall cup.
  2. Use a Stick blender starting at the bottom of cup.
  3. Work your way up till all of the oil is Emulsified.
  4. Enjoy!.
