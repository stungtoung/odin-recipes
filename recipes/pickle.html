Pickle

Pickle is a wonderful condiment that you can use in various recipes, wether on a sandwich or in a salad it's salty, savory, spicy and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber.
  • Water.
  • Vinegar.
  • Salt.
  • Sugar.
  • Spices:garlic, mustard seed, dill and chili.

What to do ?

Here is what you're going to do to make a quick dill pickle:

  1. Slice The Cucumber.
  2. Combine equal parts water and vinegar.
  3. Add salt and sugar and the Spices
  4. bring to a light simmer.
  5. Pour over The Brine on The sliced Cucumber
  6. Cover and leave it in the fridge for a couple of hours.
  7. Enjoy!.
